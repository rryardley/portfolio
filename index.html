About Me

Solving problems excites me.

I have always loved problem solving, from sudoku puzzles to physics problems, so web development was a natural path for me. Both my educational and professional backgrounds are in engineering (BS and MEng from Cornell University), and I am currently enrolled in Skillcrush's Technical Blueprint program to develop my coding skills. I am loving every minute of the program - I am a dedicated self-starter (doing this program while working as a design engineer full-time) and a fast-learner. I am a creative, out-of-the-box thinker with a high attention to detail and an eye for design. My favorite thing about web development is the combination of logic and design - being able to use both sides of my brain in one career is a dream come true!

When I'm not seeking out more coding skills to learn, I love snuggling up with my family and binging our favorite shows, or venturing out to a local brewery for a pint!
