About Me

Hi there! Thanks for dropping by.

I am a Web Designer and Developer with a background in engineering and art. I graduated from Cornell University with a BS and MEng in Engineering in 2014. I have taken art classes through high school, college, and post-grad life. I love designing and developing websites because it combines my love of logic and problem-solving with my passion for art and design.

When I'm not seeking out more coding skills to learn, I love snuggling up with my family and binging our favorite shows, or venturing out to a local brewery for a pint!

I offer custom websites from scratch or from templates. I can also custom code WordPress sites for clients who wish to edit their content regularly. For those with existing sites that just need some editing, I can perform a redesign, including making a desktop site responsive (optimized for mobile and tablet). I enjoy working with clients who want to highlight and enhance their unique brand through their website. If that’s you, please get in touch!
